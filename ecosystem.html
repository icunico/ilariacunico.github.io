Ecosystem River Dynamics

This page presents my research on river ecosystem dynamics, focusing on nonlinear interactions between flow, sediment, and vegetation.

The work highlights how biogeomorphic feedbacks can generate complex and potentially chaotic behavior in river systems, limiting long-term predictability.
