About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the OGS, focusing on ecological modeling, marine trophic networks, and environmental engineering. I study the interactions between atmosphere, land, and ocean systems to understand and predict environmental changes.

Research

My research interests include:

  • Marine trophic networks (phytoplankton, zooplankton)
  • Subseasonal climate predictability
  • Ecological modeling of multi-stability and feedback loops
  • Surface temperature and precipitation prediction skill
